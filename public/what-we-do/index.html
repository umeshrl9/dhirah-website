OUTREACH PROJECTS

for the youth, by the youth

DISCOVER YOUR SELF

In a world of constant distractions, staying focused and connected to yourself is tough. Many chase what’s flashy, only to feel lost or burnt out. Discover Your Self (DYS) is a 6-session certificate course that helps you pause, reflect, and rediscover what truly matters.

It’s short. It’s sharp. And it’s something you can’t afford to miss.

POSITIVE THINKER

Positive Thinker is a transformative program that empowers youth to be focused, responsible, and value-driven. Through practical sessions and team activities, it builds time management, leadership, and collaboration skills—helping you grow, lead, and serve with purpose.

Whether you're aiming to lead, serve, or simply grow—Positive Thinker equips you to rise, responsibly.

OASIS

Oasis is a refreshing break from the academic grind—where real talk, creativity, and connection come alive. With inspiring alumni stories, debates, drama, music, fun games, and great food, it’s where college life meets deeper purpose. Add to that delicious food, fun games...

And the kind of people who think beyond marks (though a 9 CGPA doesn’t hurt!)

EUPHORIA

Euphoria is where creativity finds its voice and purpose. From debates and skits to music, drama, and photography, it’s a vibrant space where students explore their talents and channel them toward something meaningful.

More than an event, Euphoria is an experience
