WHAT WE DO

Unlock the full potential of your university experience with KCSOC. Experience the synergy of like-minded individuals and discover new values to help you grow in your daily life.

Weekly events

Weekly events take place in over 20 universities across the country. They vary from discussions, talks and debates where you explore the deeper and important questions of life. It’s a chance to be a part of a tight knit community and make lasting friendships.

Retreats

Retreats are a fun and uplifting escape where we dive into a ton of live group activities. It’s a chance to recharge, make new friends, and grow spiritually in a vibrant, supportive community.

Flagship events, just for you

Flagship events are a chance to connect with the wider community. We bring in special guest speakers and celebrities, making it a highlight of an evening. These are not to be missed!
