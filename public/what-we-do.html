OUTREACH PROJECTS

for the youth, by the youth

DISCOVER YOUR SELF

In a world full of distractions, staying focused—and truly connected to yourself—is harder than ever. Many young minds chase what's loud and flashy, only to feel empty, confused, or burnt out. Discover Your Self (DYS) is a powerful, eye-opening 6-session certificate course designed to help you pause, reflect, and reconnect with what really matters. It's not about preaching—it's about real conversations, real clarity, and real growth. It’s short. It’s sharp. And it’s something you can’t afford to miss.

POSITIVE THINKER

Positive Thinker is a transformative program crafted to shape focused, responsible, and value-driven youth. It offers practical guidance on managing time effectively, cultivating authentic leadership, and channelling creativity toward meaningful social impact. Through engaging sessions and collective activities, students learn how to work in teams, share responsibilities, and contribute to a purpose beyond themselves Whether you're aiming to lead, serve, or simply grow—Positive Thinker equips you to rise, responsibly.

OASIS

Oasis is your refreshing escape from the academic grind—a space where college life meets real talk, creativity, and connection. From alumni sharing eye-opening lessons from campus and corporate life, to thought-provoking debates, powerful drama performances, and soul-soothing music—Oasis brings it all together. Add to that delicious food, fun games, and the kind of people who think beyond marks (though a 9 CGPA doesn’t hurt!)

EUPHORIA

Euphoria is where creativity finds its voice and purpose. From debates and skits to music, drama, and photography, it’s a vibrant space where students explore their talents and channel them toward something meaningful. What begins as a stage for expression soon transforms into a place that feels like home—where friendships grow, peer support flourishes, and everyone becomes part of something bigger. More than an event, Euphoria is an experience
